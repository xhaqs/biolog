🐾 Using Biolog
QUICK GUIDE
📷 Capture & Identify
Tap the viewfinder to take a photo, or upload one. Point at a plant or animal for an instant AI species ID — common name, Latin name, and local/vernacular name where known.
📸 Multi-Angle Capture
After your first photo, you can add up to 2 more angles before identifying — this improves accuracy for tricky specimens. Tap "Identify Now" whenever you're ready.
📵 Works Offline
No signal? Keep capturing — sightings queue safely on your device and automatically identify + sync the moment you're back online. An amber banner shows pending items.
